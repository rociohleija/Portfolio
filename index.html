Mexican Cuisine App

One of the ways to escape to my wonderful creations is by cooking. Listening to music and dancing while I'm making a delicious meal is my go-to for relaxation. I love to do it as loved ones come together and gather and make memories.

I Love Tortas App

Are you a fan of sandwiches, then maybe a torta can be one of your favorites? Eating a torta on the sidelines of a market, the smoke of gorditas being fried, and the freshness of the sweet aroma of fruits and vegetables while buying groceries to take to the village, are some of my childhood memories. The crispiness of the bread with many combinations and choices of meat, is de-li-cious. If you would like to know how to make a loma de torta, click at the bottom and I invite you to make one!
